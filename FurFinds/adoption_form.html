Pet Adoption Process & Guidelines

Adopting a pet is a deeply rewarding, lifelong commitment. Our goal is to connect wonderful animals with loving, responsible homes. We have a screening process in place to ensure the best possible match for both the pet and the adopter.

Requirements for Pet Adopters

  • Be 18 years or older with valid photo ID.
  • Provide current proof of residence.
  • If renting, provide written permission from your landlord allowing pets.
  • Demonstrate a stable living situation and the financial ability to cover lifelong vet care and needs.

Adoption Process

  • Submit a detailed application form online or in person.
  • Our team will review your application and conduct an interview (phone/video/in-person).
  • Interact with the pet at our facility. A home visit or photos of your home may be required.
  • Sign the adoption contract, pay the non-refundable fee, and take your pet home.

Post-Adoption Responsibilities

By adopting a pet, you agree to:

  • Ensure adequate food, shelter, water, and exercise.
  • Provide necessary annual vaccinations and medical attention.
  • Follow terms regarding spay/neuter, microchipping, and restrictions on declawing or rehoming without our consent.
  • If you can no longer care for the pet, you must return the animal to us.
