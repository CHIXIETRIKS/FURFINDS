How Can We Help You

1. How does the adoption process work?
A quick walkthrough of applying, meeting the pet, approval, and bringing them home.
2. What are the requirements to adopt a pet?
Basic age requirement, ID, home check (if any), and agreement to care standards.
3. How much is the adoption fee?
A simple breakdown of fees and what they usually cover, like vaccines or spaying/neutering.
4. Are the pets vaccinated and checked by a vet?
Information about health checks, vaccinations, and medical records.
5. Can I adopt if I have other pets at home?
Guidance on introducing pets to each other and any compatibility notes.
6. Can we return the pet if it doesn't work out?
Your return policy or steps for rehoming through the shelter.
7. How long does the approval process take?
Give an estimated time so adopters know what to expect.
8. Do you allow reservations for pets?
Explain if you accept holds or if it's first-come-first-served.
9. Can I foster instead of adopting?
Short info about fostering and how to apply if they're not ready for full adoption.
10. What should I prepare before bringing my new pet home?
Starter tips like food, crates, litter, toys, and a calm space to settle in.
11. Do you offer support after adoption?
Mention if you provide check-ins, advice, or training tips.
12. What if the pet has special needs?
Guidance for adopting seniors or pets with medical or behavioral needs.
